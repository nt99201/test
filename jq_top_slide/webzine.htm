  • Everland, the World of Blossoms

    Enjoy the fragrances of various spring flowers in a walk along the flower tour, from the
    cherry blossom path to the tulip garden!

    Flower Magic Tree

    Visit a large tulip tree that is 13m tall, and enjoy flower rains during the day and magical lights at night.

    Four Seasons Garden

    Over 300 pinwheels spin at the four seasons garden, which is filled with fragrances and beautiful tulips.
    Don’t miss the pinwheels spinning in the spring breeze!

    Flower Tour for Drivers

    Enjoy the fantastic driving tour, filled with flowers and trees from the gate of Everland Resort!

  • Entertainment

    Enjoy Everland’s entertainment performances!
    We have added fun and touching moments to celebrate the 35th anniversary of Everland!

    Carnival Fantasy Parade (3/18~)

    This ultra-long parade features 13 floats and over 130 performers.
    The parade is 670m long, and brings major global festivals from Rio, Venice and the Caribbean to Everland!

    Dream of Laciun (3/25~)

    A multimedia show, complete with light and fire and presented by Laciun and Phoenix, the peacekeepers!
    Don’t miss the five-minute finale, with more than 6,000 fireworks!

    Carnival Elysion (3/25~)

    A fantastic performance, combining European dances and a circus!
    Bolero, a dance of the heavens, passionate gypsy dances and amazing silk dances are waiting for you!
    You can also enjoy various circus performers along with the dances

    Moonlight Parade (3/18~)

    The special night parade is completed with 1 million lamps to create a world of magic and fairy tales!
